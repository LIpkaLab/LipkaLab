Dr. Alex Lipka

Principal Investigator

Dr. Lipka leads the Lipka Lab, specializing in quantitative genetics for crops. His background is in applied statistics, and really enjoys using this background to develop more robust genotype-to-phenotype approaches. Outside of work, Dr. Lipka enjoys hanging out with his family, going long-distance running, and playing piano and guitar. Dr. Lipka tries to visit a different national park each summer.
