About the Lab

The Lipka Lab is a quantitative genetics lab that applies state-of-the-art statistical approaches to quantify genotype-to-phenotype associations in crops. This work helps ensure that advances in phenotyping and sequencing crops translate to meaningful gains in both breeding practices and understanding of how and why genetic architectures evolve. Ultimately, the Lipka Lab seeks to do its part in dissecting the genotype-to-phenotype landscape and guaranteeing food security in the challenging world humanity faces in the future.
